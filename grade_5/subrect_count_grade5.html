Sub-Rectangle Counting

Grade 5  ·  Competition  ·  Single-point, both/one/neither, three-point inclusion-exclusion

Single Point Both Points One Not Other Neither Three Points Competition

Quick Reference

  • Total sub-rectangles in an n×m grid: \(\frac{n(n+1)}{2} \times \frac{m(m+1)}{2}\).
  • Single point A at (r, c): count = \(r \times (n-r+1) \times c \times (m-c+1)\). Choose a left wall ≤ c, right wall ≥ c, top wall ≤ r, bottom wall ≥ r.
  • Both A and B: bounding-box formula — \(\min(r_A,r_B)\times(n-\max(r_A,r_B)+1)\times\min(c_A,c_B)\times(m-\max(c_A,c_B)+1)\).
  • A but not B: contain(A) − contain(A∩B).
  • Neither A nor B: Total − contain(A) − contain(B) + contain(A∩B).
  • A or B, but not C: |A∪B| − |(A∪B)∩C| = (contA+contB−contAB) − (contAC+contBC−contABC).
