River Boat Problems

Grade 5  ·  Competition  ·  Upstream, downstream, round-trip, word problems, and water-frame trick

Speed Model One Trip Two Equations Time Compare Word Probs Competition
  • Two unknowns: v = boat speed in still water, u = current speed (always u < v).
  • Downstream (with current): speed = v + u.
  • Upstream (against current): speed = v − u.
  • Recover v and u from two speeds: v = (vdown + vup) ÷ 2,   u = (vdown − vup) ÷ 2.
  • Two-trip system: same distance d, times t↓ and t↑ → v+u = d/t↓, v−u = d/t↑. Solve by adding/subtracting.
  • Water-frame trick: in the river's reference frame the current disappears. A floating object is stationary; the boat's out-and-back times are equal. Distance drifted = current × total time.
  • Same-time ratio: if x km downstream = y km upstream (same time), then v : u = (x+y) : (x−y).
