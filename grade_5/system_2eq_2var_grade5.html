System of Two Linear Equations

Grade 5  ·  Solve two-unknown problems using substitution and elimination

Concepts Substitution Elimination Scale & Eliminate Word Problems Strategy & Verify

Two Methods

  • Substitution: From one equation, express x (or y) in terms of the other. Substitute into the second equation to get one variable, then back-substitute.
  • Elimination: Add or subtract equations to cancel one variable. If coefficients don't match, scale one or both equations first.

Choosing the Method

  • A coefficient is 1? → Substitution is fast.
  • One variable has equal coefficients? → Subtract directly (no scaling).
  • No equal coefficients? → Scale one or both equations, then eliminate.
  • Always verify: substitute (x, y) back into both original equations.
