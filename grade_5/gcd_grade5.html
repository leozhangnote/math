Greatest Common Divisor (GCD)

Grade 5  ·  Factors, listing method, prime factorization, Euclidean algorithm, and word problems

Factors Listing Prime Fact. Euclidean Word Problems Coprime
  • Factor: a divides b evenly (no remainder). List factor pairs from 1 up to √b.
  • GCD by listing: list all factors of each number, find common ones, pick the largest.
  • GCD by prime factorization: factor each number; for each common prime, take the minimum exponent; multiply.
  • Euclidean algorithm: GCD(a, b) = GCD(b, a mod b). Repeat until remainder = 0; GCD = last non-zero remainder.
  • Word problem signal: "equal pieces, no waste / no leftovers / largest equal share" → GCD.
  • Coprime: GCD(a, b) = 1. After dividing both by GCD d, the quotients m and n satisfy GCD(m, n) = 1.
