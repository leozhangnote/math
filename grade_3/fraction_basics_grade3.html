Parts of a Whole Unit Fractions Comparing Fractions

What is a fraction?

A fraction shows part of a whole that has been divided into equal parts.

Parts of a fraction

  • Numerator (top) — how many parts you have or are counting.
  • Denominator (bottom) — how many equal parts the whole is divided into.

Key unit fractions to know

  • 1/2 — one out of 2 equal parts (a half)
  • 1/3 — one out of 3 equal parts (a third)
  • 1/4 — one out of 4 equal parts (a quarter)
  • 1/6 and 1/8 — one out of 6 or 8 equal parts

Comparing tips

  • Same bottom? Bigger top = bigger fraction. (3/8 > 1/8)
  • Same top? Bigger bottom = smaller fraction. (1/3 > 1/6)

Golden rule

All parts must be equal in size before you can write a fraction!
