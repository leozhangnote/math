Average Formulas

  • Average = Sum ÷ Count
  • Sum = Average × Count ← flip this to find missing values
  • Missing = Avg × n − (sum of known values)
  • Add one value: New avg = (Old avg × n + new value) ÷ (n + 1)
  • Remove one value: New avg = (Old avg × n − removed) ÷ (n − 1)
  • Combine two groups: (n₁ × A₁ + n₂ × A₂) ÷ (n₁ + n₂)
  • Overlap element: x = Sum₁ + Sum₂ − Total Sum
  • Average speed (equal distance): 2v₁v₂ ÷ (v₁ + v₂) — NOT (v₁+v₂)÷2
