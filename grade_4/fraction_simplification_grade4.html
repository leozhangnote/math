Fraction Simplification

Grade 4  ·  Arithmetic  ·  Reduce fractions to lowest terms using the GCD.

Reduce by GCD Fraction Division Parentheses First Variable Factors Nested Fractions

Core rules

  • Divide top & bottom by their GCD to simplify
  • Evaluate parentheses before simplifying
  • For division: flip the second fraction, then simplify
  • Only cancel factors, never terms in a sum
  • Nested fractions: treat inner ÷ as multiply-by-reciprocal
